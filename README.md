### **FableVerse**

FableVerse is an open-source project dedicated to providing a privacy-respecting and offline solution for managing and playing both audiobooks and music. Designed to work entirely offline on mobile devices, FableVerse ensures that your personal data remains secure and is never shared with third parties. It’s available for download on F-Droid, making it accessible to all users who care about privacy and control over their media.

## Key Features:
**Audiobook Features:**
* Sleeping Timer: Set a timer to automatically stop playback after a set duration, perfect for bedtime listening.
* Progress Tracking: Keep track of your listening progress and resume from where you left off, with automatic save points.
* Separate Audiobook Folders: Manage your audiobooks in a dedicated space, separate from your music collection, for better organization.

**Music Features:**
* Playlists: Create and manage custom playlists to curate your music collection.
* Genres: Organize your music by genre for easier navigation.
* Albums: Enjoy album-based listening with album art support.

**Player:**
A fully integrated audio player capable of playing both music and audiobooks, with a simple and intuitive interface designed for easy use.
FableVerse works seamlessly with your existing MP3 files, providing an all-in-one solution for users who prefer to store and manage their media offline.

## Why FableVerse?
* Privacy Focused: No data collection, no tracking—your listening habits are yours and yours alone.
* Offline First: Works fully offline, so you can enjoy your content without needing an internet connection.
* F-Droid Availability: Easily install and update via F-Droid, the trusted platform for open-source Android apps.
